Hi, I'm Karan!

I am a final year DPhil (PhD) student in Computer Science at the University of Oxford, working in the Oxford Control and Verification group under the supervision of Alessandro Abate.

My research focuses on robust, computationally tractable optimisation methods for large-scale power systems. I coordinate populations of controllable domestic devices, such as EVs and heat pumps, to unlock their flexibility for a sustainable energy grid.

I care a lot about building sustainable energy systems. Prior to my PhD, I worked at National Grid ET as a data scientist, and at Electron as a software engineer. I also collaborated with Open Climate Fix on the development of a mobile app, and I interned at CCFE working on nuclear fusion research.
